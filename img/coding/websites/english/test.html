The English Division

The English Division, with more than 100 full-time faculty, offers courses in four areas: Composition and Rhetoric, Literature, Creative Writing, and Reading.


Faculty Spotlight

The English Division Presents Professor Liam Madden:

Liam Madden, Assistant Professor of English and Humanities, serves as Distinguished Chair of the Southern Academy for Literary Arts & Scholarly Research. He received his M.A. in English from Virginia Commonwealth University (1993). He has served as a contributing editor for The Chattahoochee Review and as editor of Sinescope, an online journal of the arts. His poems and short stories have appeared in The Corn Creek Review, Hobart Park, and other regional publications.

Liam previously worked on the staff of the Edgar Allan Poe Museum, Richmond VA and the Margaret Mitchell House and Museum, Atlanta GA. He was the editor of A Time to Reconcile, a chronicle of the civil rights movement in Georgia and Epic Grandeur, a comparative study of Japanese and Western epic literature.
