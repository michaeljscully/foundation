Day Hall, Atlanta Botanical Garden.

The Townsend Prize for Fiction.

The program schedule is as follows:

5:00 pm

Tour of Atlanta Botanical Garden

6:30 pm

Day Hall doors open; Book sales and signing with Townsend Prize-nominated authors

7:00 pm

Reception with jazz band and buffet

7:30 pm

Keynote address by Ann Beattie and awards ceremony

April 26, 5:00-8:30 pm
Day Hall, Atlanta Botanical Garden.

GPC College-wide Creative Writing Awards
Held in conjunction with the Townsend Prize for Fiction. Sponsored by Creative License. General admission ticket is $40; tickets for prize-winning students and their parents are provided by GPC's Southern Academy.
