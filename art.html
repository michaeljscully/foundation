About Me

Who am I, anyway?

I’m a 2009 graduate of the University of Notre Dame, majoring in both Graphic Design and Computer Applications.

I’ve worked as an in-house graphic designer and web developer, and I’ve been out on my own as a freelancer doing everything from creative direction to working on video games.

I love to work on the cutting edge and challenge myself with new ideas and new technologies.
